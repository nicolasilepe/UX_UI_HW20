Product

This app is used for users to plan activities or trips with their friends, working around everyone’s schedule. It offers notifications about upcoming events and suggests local activities based on preferences.

Goals

  • Define user needs
  • Identify scheduling conflicts
  • Establish effective group communication
  • Find common ground for group decisions

Methodology

Surveys and interviews were conducted to collect data essential to the project’s research.

Problem

My Friends and I was designed to make group scheduling easier. However, users face challenges in managing scheduling goals. How might we improve the app to meet these needs?
